Highlights

This resort boasts an impressive range of high-quality amenities, making it a popular choice for both couples and families. It has received multiple awards and spans across 22 acres of lush beachfront gardens along Bamburi Beach, which is renowned as one of the finest beaches on the north coast of Mombasa. The resort offers a wide array of facilities catering to all age groups, including five swimming pools, tennis courts, a gym, and volleyball courts. Additionally, there are clubs for both children and teenagers to keep them engaged and entertained. Located on a beautiful beach, the resort provides an extensive selection of water sports for guests to enjoy. For those seeking relaxation, the Tulia Spa offers delightful treatments. A diverse range of restaurants caters to various tastes, and several bars serve delicious beverages. Guests can also visit the disco, which hosts regular live entertainment. The resort is conveniently located approximately 40 minutes away from Mombasa airport.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • DAY 1 NAIROBI TO MOMBASA VIA SGR

    NAIROBI TO MOMBASA VIA SGR The transfer to Nairobi SGR Station at Syokimau will be on your own arrangement. You are required to depart from Nairobi as per the SGR timing booked. The train ride to Mombasa is quite exciting as you get to spot elephants, zebras and giraffes as you pass through Tsavo National Park. As soon as you arrive to the Mombasa Terminus you will meet our trusted and reliable Bonfire Agent. He will help you with your luggage and drive you from the Mombasa Terminus to your hotel. As soon as you check in, you can sit back and relax. Soak up the sun, as you enjoy the pool, exciting beach activities, take a nap between two palm trees or if you'd like you can head out and explore the city of Mombasa. Meals And Drinks As Per The Hotel Chosen

  • DAY 2 ENJOYING MOMBASA

    If you're a beach-filled vacation person, you will especially love this day. Fill your time with nothing but pool, ocean, drinks and relaxation. To make the most out of your experience in Mombasa, how about taking part in a series of fun and exciting beach activities. There are various water sports around Mombasa including boat riding, jet-ski, kite-surfing and among others offered at your own cost. Mombasa has a ton of awesome spots giving you the perfect balance of entertainment and letting you relax. They also have exciting children's activities to keep the most important member of the family happy.

  • DAY 3 IN MOMBASA THEN BACK TO NAIROBI

    This will be your last vacation day in Mombasa. After breakfast enjoy a few moments at the hotel. Go to the beach, sip a cool drink and soak in the sun. Our Bonfire Representative will drive to the hotel, pick you and take you to the SGR Station (Mombasa Terminus). We guarantee that you will arrive at the station right on time for the departure to Nairobi. The train ride to Nairobi offers beautiful scenery-a smooth transition from your Mombasa holiday back to the city. As soon as you get to the city, the transfer back to your home will be as per your own arrangement.
