Highlights

Mauritius is an Indian Ocean Island nation and is known for its beaches, lagoons, and reefs. The mountainous interior includes Black River Gorges, a National Park with rainforests, waterfalls, hiking trails, and wildlife like the flying fox. Capital Port Louis has sites such as the Champs de Mars horse track, Eureka plantation house, and 18th-century Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam Botanical Gardens

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival At Mauritius

    As you arrive in Mauritius, the Bonfire Adventures will greet you at the airport and escort you to the resort. The rest of the day would be free for you to explore the property and peacefully relax in the serene Mauritian aura. By the fall of the evening, a delectable dinner will be served to you at the hotel's main restaurant. Rejoice a gala dinner followed by a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Excursion To Ile Aux Cerfs Island Mauritius

    Enjoy delicious bites at breakfast and set off for an expedition to the picturesque lagoon Ile Aux Cerfs. Take a walk around its golden stretches of beautiful beaches and be mesmerized with the stunning ocean vistas. Take a swim in the island's gin-clear waters. And if that sets your tickling adventure bones, you can opt for the optional water activities in Mauritius such as snorkeling, tube ride, parasailing, water skiing and undersea walk at an additional cost. Once you return to the hotel, the evening is yours to relax on the white shores. Add to the whole experience with a lavish dinner in the tranquil night.

  • Day At Leisure At (Casela Nature Park) Mauritius

    Wake up early to catch the sun spread its beams in the morning at the ocean side and set the mood for the rest of the day free for leisure. Enjoy your breakfast and gear up for a leisure day. Enjoy an exciting excursion in the glass-bottom boat to catch glimpses of the tropical underwater world or enjoy other watersports like kayaking, sailing or windsurfing. You can visit the famour Casela Naature Park and indulge in exciting activities ranging from coming face to face with the King of The Jungle or Quad Biking

  • MAURITIUS: NORTH ISLAND TOUR

    Prepare to leave for a tour of the capital city Port Louis in the Northern Region of Mauritius in coach and enjoy you day. The tour will take you through Fort Adelaide which features an extensive view of Champs de Mars racecourse and explore other attraction in Mauritius. Drive through the impeccable roads and witness the city from up close. On reaching Le Caudan Waterfront, you can go shopping at the complex & the craft markets. Seated in one of the restaurants of the hotel at dinner time, choose from its wide range of delicacies. Soak in the Mauritian air and if you'd like, enjoy a late-night walk along the beautiful beach.

  • MAURITIUS: LAGOON OVER THE SOUTH ISLANDS

    The breathtaking South Islands is a spot that you must explore when visiting Mauritius. Enjoy the breathtaking landscapes as you explore the monuments and the great heritage of Mauritius. Tour the Cavendish Bridge, National Museum, Le Souffleur, Blue Bay and La Roche Qui Pleure.

  • Day 01 Arrival Nairobi

    Approximately 22kms, takes approximately 1 hour's drive depending on traffic Bonfire Adventures guide driver will warmly meet you on arrival at the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, transfer you to the Sarova Stanley Hotel. Check-in then we brief you on what will hopefully be your best holiday in Africa. Enjoy overnight in Standard rooms. You are booked on Bed & Breakfast

  • Day 02 Nairobi - Amboseli National Park

    Approximately 240kms, takes approximately 4.5 hours' drive depending on traffic Enjoy breakfast then we drive you to the Amboseli National Park arriving at the Amboseli Serena Safari Lodge for check-in then lunch. Amboseli is great for a Kenya safari. Enjoy an afternoon game viewing drive. Varied wildlife includes giraffes, zebras, cheetahs and hundreds of bird species. The western section is dominated by vast Lake Amboseli, which is dry outside the rainy season. Later return back to the camp for dinner and overnight.

  • Day 03 Amboseli National Park

    Enjoy early morning game viewing drive the return back to the lodge for breakfast. Mid-morning is at leisure. If the weather permits you will enjoy breath-taking views of the snow-capped Mount Kilimanjaro. It is from the Amboseli where some of the most captivating wildlife photos with Mount Kilimanjaro in the background have been taken. Enjoy dinner and overnight at the Amboseli Serena Safari Lodge.

  • Day 04 Amboseli - Lake Nakuru National Park

    Approximately 390kms, takes approximately 7.5 hours' drive depending on traffic Enjoy breakfast then with packed lunch we drive you to the Lake Nakuru national park with a brief stop at Great Rift Valley view point. Enjoy an afternoon game viewing drive. The park is famous for the abundant greater and lesser flamingoes (when the later levels on the lake are low). Wildlife here is bountiful including lion, cheetah, leopard, and hyenas. Rothschild giraffe as well as black and white rhino are also found here are. Lake Nakuru National Park is an ornithological paradise for bird lovers as well. Later drive to the Sarova Lion Hill Lodge for check-in. Enjoy dinner and overnight.

  • Day 05 Lake Nakuru National Park - Masai Mara National Reserve

    Approximately 270kms, takes approximately 6 hours' drive depending on traffic Enjoy breakfast then we pick and drive you to the Masai Mara National Reserve, the best Kenya safaris destination. This famous game reserve is one of the best places for a Safari in Africa. You will be arriving at the Emayian Luxury Camp for lunch. Proceed on an afternoon game viewing drive. Later return back to the camp for dinner and overnight.

  • Day 06 Masai Mara National Reserve

    Enjoy breakfast then depart with packed lunch for a full day of game viewing drives. You can also opt for a morning and afternoon game viewing drives with hot lunch at the camp. This famous game reserve is world famous for the spectacular wildebeest migration between July through to September each year, the abundant wildlife including the 'Big five' of Africa. The reserve is surrounded by Masai traditional home-steads and so at a small extra cost you may want to experience a cultural visit to one of them. Enjoy dinner and overnight at the Emayian Luxury Camp.

  • Day 07 Masai Mara National Reserve - Lake Naivasha

    Approximately 265kms, takes approximately 6 hours' drive depending on traffic Enjoy breakfast then we drive you through the floor of the Rift Valley to Naivasha. You will be arriving at the Lake Naivasha Sopa Lodge for lunch. Enjoy afternoon at leisure or at an extra cost you will have a one-hour boat ride in the lake or better still, to the crescent island. Enjoy diner and overnight.

  • Day 08 Departure

    Approximately 100kms, takes approximately 2.5 hours' drive depending on traffic Enjoy breakfast then check-out. We drive you back to Nairobi. Enjoy lunch at the Carnivore restaurant then our guide driver will drop you at the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your departure flight. We at Bonfire Adventures sincerely thank you for traveling with us on your Best Holiday in Africa.
